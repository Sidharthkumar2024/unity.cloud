Frequently asked questions

Do tokens expire?

Monthly tokens reset with each billing cycle. Bulk pack credits do not expire.

Which models are included?

All public routing targets connected to your workspace are included unless a custom policy excludes them.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. Contact the access team and your next cycle can move to the plan that fits your usage.

How do I get started?

Request access, receive your key, and connect an OpenAI-compatible or Anthropic-compatible client.
